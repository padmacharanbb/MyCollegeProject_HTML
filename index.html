ABOUT TEAM RAGE FARMERS

Rage Farmers is a dynamic student-led agri-tech team driven by innovation, sustainability, and real-world impact. Comprising students from agriculture and engineering backgrounds, we blend skills in IoT, full-stack development, SolidWorks design, and agronomy to create smart, affordable solutions for modern farming challenges. Our projects focus on biodegradable technologies, precision farming tools, and eco-friendly alternatives like plant-based hydrogels and cellulose biofilms. With hands-on prototyping and field testing, we ensure our innovations are practical, scalable, and farmer-friendly. Selected as finalists for TIFAN 2025, we are proud to represent our institute and compete for the Best Innovation Award. Rage Farmers is committed to building a future where technology empowers every farmer and drives sustainable growth in Indian agriculture.
